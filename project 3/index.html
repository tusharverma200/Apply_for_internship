Department: Digital & Technical Innovation

StoryCorps’ Digital & Technical Innovation department is a lean and dedicated team of professionals who work very closely with all departments in our organization to build and maintain StoryCorps’ award winning web and app technologies. The Web Development student intern will assist in updating and editing content for StoryCorps.org and other sites, creating and editing web page templates, and assisting in other web-related projects as necessary.

    Projects may include, but will not be limited to:

  • Write custom HTML, PHP, CSS and JavaScript for existing websites and applications.

  • Update and edit website content, posts and pages.
  • Provide technical support related to web-based systems to internal teams.
  • Test and give feedback on new and existing technologies.
  • Create prototypes and experiment with new technologies and features as assigned.
